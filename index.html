About Us

We are a family owned and operated New England retail pizza delivery and carry out company. We have been servicing the Burlington, Vermont area with two stores for 20 years and opened a third store in Portland, Maine in 2007.

We are proud to have received “Best Pizza” recognition with numerous awards. Our family members have created all of Leonardo’s pizza recipes, taking pride in the development of traditional, vegetarian and gourmet pizzas. We have worked hard over the years to be creative and innovative in our flavor combinations.
